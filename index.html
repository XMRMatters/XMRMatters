Our Mission

XMRMatters is a new peer-to-peer exchange platform dedicated to privacy, freedom, and user empowerment. We believe in the fundamental right to financial privacy and the power of decentralized, person-to-person transactions.

Built on the principles that made LocalMonero a trusted community hub, we're creating a platform where users can trade directly with each other, maintaining control over their privacy and their assets.

Launch Date: December 1st, 2025 at 12:00 AM UTC
